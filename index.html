01

The Mission

Kroger walked back its cage-free egg commitment. CRFB's campaign puts that decision at the feet of the eight individuals with the power to reverse it - the board of directors. Each director has a dedicated microsite. Our job is to make those pages unavoidable whenever those names are searched.

These are name-interception pages, not traffic plays. The exact-match domains (MeetRonSargent.com, WhoIsKevinBrown.com) are built precisely for navigational and "who is X" intent. Search volume is low by design - the value is who sees it, not how many.

Who we are putting these pages in front of
The Directors

People search their own names. Every time a director does, the campaign is waiting.

Journalists

Reporters covering Kroger vet board members by name - and cite what ranks.

Investors

Proxy advisors and institutional investors research governance before votes.

Recruiters

Board seats are reputational assets. Search results follow directors to their next one.

Built to last: everything in this campaign is white-hat authority building - real placements on real sites. No tactics that spike for a month and get filtered out. If Kroger's side starts spending to drown this out, the equity we build keeps compounding; theirs starts from zero.
02

What We're Building

Five workstreams, all included in this engagement. Off-site authority is the backbone; everything else multiplies it.

Off-Site Authority Links

5-10 editorial backlinks per microsite (40-80 total) in the DA 40-49 band, anchored to each director's name and company-qualified terms. The range is deliberate: a small keyword universe means anchor text has to rotate, and as authority banks we can shift to fewer, higher-DA placements rather than repeating anchors monthly.

Brand-Demand Campaigns

Organic campaigns that lift branded search volume for each director's name. This is the engine that makes exact-match domains rank - search engines reward domains that people actually look for.

OTTO Optimization $1,000On the House

Our AI optimization engine, auto-installed and run across all eight microsites - page titles, meta descriptions, header structure, and schema, dialed in automatically. Served so AI crawlers that skip client-side JavaScript still see the fully optimized pages. There is no point building links to pages that are not optimized to receive them - so this is included at no cost.

Cloud Stacks Included · No Charge

Additional distributed authority signals layered onto each of the eight properties - extra reinforcement we are throwing in because the mission matters to us.

Digital PR Exploratory

We will attempt press-release distribution for the campaign. Straight talk: this space is contested, and some publisher networks may decline the content. Where one network says no, we route to another - and we report exactly what landed.

Month-One Fast Start

Manual link building begins immediately while our SEO team completes deep keyword research in parallel. You do not wait for a strategy deck to see placements - the campaign is moving inside the first week and fully deployed within the 15-30 day rollout window.

How we prioritize
